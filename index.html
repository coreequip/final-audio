About Me

Hi, my name is Rolf Maier Bode. Just after computer programming as a teen (C64 “Demos”) I released my first record in 1991, and in the mid-90s under the RMB tag, wrote, produced and performed melodic techno. Six of my tunes hit the German top 30, and one was awarded a gold record.

In 2005, I founded loudbranding offering composition, production and sound design for advertising and media - including TVCs, Dolby 5.1 cinema surround sound, sonic branding, scores and sound design for BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Maserati, Pringles, MARS, the ESA and Zeiss.

But I have always loved working with films and imagery. So, with composed music on ads now mostly gone, I founded final.audio, so I can bring professional post-production audio to all media producers at very affordable rates.
